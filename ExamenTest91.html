1: In a low-power, solar-powered environmental monitoring system with strict energy constraints, which microcontroller configuration is most appropriate? [Tema 5Teacher]

  • A) A 16-bit RISC microcontroller running at 100% duty cycle.
  • B) An 8-bit AVR microcontroller with sleep modes and disabled ADC during idle.
