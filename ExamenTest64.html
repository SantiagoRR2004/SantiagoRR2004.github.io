1: Which of the following business motivations drive the convergence of IoT and cloud computing? [Tema 7Teacher]

  • A) Avoidance of CAPEX in early IoT start-ups.
  • B) Elimination of interoperability challenges across devices.
  • C) Support for computationally intensive AI services.
  • D) The need for device-specific control interfaces.
  • E) Access to development tools regardless of location.
