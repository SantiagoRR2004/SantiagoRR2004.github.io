1: What is the primary role of the CPwE (Converged Plantwide Ethernet) architecture in manufacturing? [Tema 8Teacher]

  • A) Integrate IACS applications with enterprise IT systems over a secure Ethernet network.
  • B) Standardize all manufacturing equipment to use Wi-Fi and Bluetooth.
  • C) Replace proprietary automation protocols with generic TCP/IP stacks.
