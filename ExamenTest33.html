1: Which of the following are incorrect assumptions about IoT-enabled smart manufacturing? [Tema 8Teacher]

  • A) Usage data from products can inform future design iterations.
  • B) Predictive maintenance eliminates the need for any scheduled servicing.
  • C) Customers can submit customization requests during production.
