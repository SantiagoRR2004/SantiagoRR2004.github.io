1: You are designing a vibration sensor for bridge health monitoring. It must sample high-frequency signals and transmit summaries daily. Which approach is best? [Tema 5Teacher]

  • A) Stream all raw data to the cloud continuously.
  • B) Use a PIR sensor to detect bridge traffic.
  • C) Only measure once a month and transmit immediately.
  • D) Perform FFT analysis onboard and send frequency-domain summaries.

Respuestas correctas: D
