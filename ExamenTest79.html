1: Which statements correctly describe the role of consumers in future smart energy ecosystems? [Tema 8Teacher]

  • A) Consumers passively receive electricity and have no influence on energy markets.
  • B) Consumers can engage in microtransactions by selling electricity from EVs to the grid.
  • C) Consumers may shift energy consumption behavior based on cost feedback.
  • D) All energy balancing happens at the municipal level; consumer decisions are irrelevant.
