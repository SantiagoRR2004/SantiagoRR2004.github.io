1: A manufacturing company wants to increase product customization without increasing operational downtime. Which strategy is most aligned with IIoT principles? [Tema 8Teacher]

  • A) Use CPS and IIoT to dynamically reconfigure production lines.
  • B) Outsource product customization to external vendors.
  • C) Pre-manufacture all customization variants and store them in warehouses.
