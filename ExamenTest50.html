1: You are evaluating several models for a real-time health monitoring system that detects abnormal heart rhythms from wearable data. Which of the following evaluation criteria are critical? [Tema 6Teacher]

  • A) Cross-validation accuracy above 95% on training data.
  • B) Low false negative rate to avoid missed alarms.
  • C) Real-time inference latency within acceptable bounds.
  • D) High model complexity regardless of latency constraints.
  • E) Model interpretability for medical certification and auditing.

Respuestas correctas: B, C, E
