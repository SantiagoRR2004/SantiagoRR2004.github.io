1: A logistics firm wants to ensure that food transported across a supply chain hasn’t spoiled. What is the most appropriate IoT solution to monitor this? [Tema 8Teacher]

  • A) Use RFID tags on all shipments to track position.
  • B) Analyze historical weather data from transit routes.
  • C) Install vibration sensors in delivery vehicles.
  • D) Embed temperature and humidity sensors with each shipment and log data in real-time.

Respuestas correctas: D
