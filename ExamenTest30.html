1: Which of the following applications would most accurately be classified as *ambient-assisted living (AAL)* within the IoT healthcare framework? [Tema 8Teacher]

  • B) A platform recommending exercise routines based on fitness tracking data.
  • C) A smartwatch detecting falls and automatically alerting caregivers.
